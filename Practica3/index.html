What is Rafting?

Rafting is a recreational outdoor activity which use an inflatable raft to navigate a river or other body of water. This is often done on whitewater or different degrees of rough water. Dealing with risk is often a part of the experience.

Athletes

Nicole Plagemann

Nicole Plagemann , from Germany, is known for her participation in international rafting competitions and has represented her country in various events. She has achieved so many World Championship Medals that has been awarded the best female rafting athlete in the world.

Glenn Goodrich is a well-known rafting athlete and a prominent advocate for environmental conservation, particularly focused on rivers. He has been part of the rafting community for many years and has participated in numerous international competitions, winning multiple medals and accolades.

Dane Jackson

Dane Jackson is an accomplished American kayaker and adventurer, known for his remarkable skills in whitewater kayaking and freestyle competitions. He has gained international recognition for his exceptional performances in various disciplines, including slalom, freestyle, and extreme racing.

Jesse Coombs is a recognized athlete and adventurer in the world of rafting and kayaking. Is a strong advocate for safety in rafting, educating others about best practices and the importance of being prepared for river conditions, and has inspired many to explore the world of rafting and appreciate the beauty of rivers.

Competitions

Canyon Whitewater Festival (United States)

Gore Fest provides us with an opportunity to reflect on this iconic stretch of whitewater as well as the important stewardship work that American Whitewater has done for the Upper Colorado River.

American Whitewater is also the leading representative of the recreational community on the Upper Colorado River Wild and Scenic Stakeholders group where flows for the Colorado River are negotiated by American Whitewater and our stakeholders. Gore Fest is the time to celebrate this beautiful place they work hard to protect, and an opportunity for paddlers, industry, and partners to enjoy and support their cooperative stewardship efforts.

Ganga Mahotsav (India)

The Ganga Mahotsav is one of the most exciting scenarios offered during this event is rafting. This adventure sport attracts both tourists and locals, providing a unique opportunity to experience the majesty of the Ganges while navigating its swift waters.

The festival also features various cultural activities, including music and dance performances, food stalls serving traditional cuisine, and workshops showcasing local crafts. This blend of adventure and culture makes the Ganga Mahotsav a truly unique experience, where visitors can immerse themselves in the vibrant traditions of India while enjoying the thrill of rafting on one of the world's most revered rivers.

Genil River(Spain)

The Genil River is an ideal spot for rafting and other water activities, especially during the spring and summer months. Known for its crystal-clear waters and stunning landscapes, this river attracts adventure enthusiasts looking for an exhilarating experience in nature.

Whether you're an experienced rafter or a first-timer looking for a new adventure, the Genil River promises an unforgettable experience that combines adrenaline, natural beauty, and the joy of being outdoors.
